Climate Changes

The Earth's climate has changed throughout history. Just in the last 650.000 years threre have been seven cycles of glacial advance and retreat. The abrupt end of last ice age about 7000 years ago making the begging of the modern climate area. The planet's average surface temperature has risen about 0.9 degrees Celsius since the late 19th century. A change drive increased carbon dioxide and other human-made emissions into the atmosphere.
